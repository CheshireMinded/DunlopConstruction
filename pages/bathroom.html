Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical bathroom remodel take?

Most bathroom remodels take 2-4 weeks depending on the scope and complexity. A complete remodel with structural changes may take 4-6 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do you help with bathroom design and planning?

Yes! We offer comprehensive design services including 3D renderings, material selection, and space planning. Our design process ensures your new bathroom meets both your aesthetic and functional needs.

What's included in a complete bathroom remodel?

A complete remodel typically includes vanity, toilet, shower/tub, tile work, lighting, plumbing fixtures, and any structural modifications. We'll provide a detailed scope of work during your consultation.

Do you provide warranties on your work?

Yes, we provide a 5-year craftsmanship warranty on all bathroom remodeling work. This covers installation quality, workmanship, and any issues related to our installation process.

Can you work with existing plumbing?

Absolutely! We can work with your existing plumbing layout or make modifications as needed. We'll assess your current setup during the consultation and recommend the best approach.

What areas do you serve for bathroom remodeling?

We serve Rochester Hills, Troy, Auburn Hills, Birmingham, Bloomfield Hills, and surrounding areas within a 30-mile radius of 48307. Contact us to confirm service availability in your area.
