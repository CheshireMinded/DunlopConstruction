Our Story

Founded in 2014, Dunlop Construction was born from a simple philosophy: every home deserves the highest level of craftsmanship, attention to detail, and professional service.

What started as a small team with big dreams has grown into one of Greater Rochester Hills' most trusted names in home improvement. We specialize in flooring, kitchen and bathroom remodeling, and interior and exterior painting—always with an unwavering commitment to quality.

Our minimalist approach doesn't mean simple—it means purposeful. Every line, every finish, every detail serves a purpose in creating spaces that are both beautiful and functional. We believe that luxury isn't about excess; it's about perfection in every detail.
