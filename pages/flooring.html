Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical flooring installation take?

Most flooring installations take 1-3 days depending on the size and complexity. Hardwood installations may take 3-5 days including finishing time. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do you help with flooring selection and design?

Yes! We offer complimentary design consultations to help you choose the perfect flooring for your space, lifestyle, and budget. We can show you samples and provide recommendations based on your specific needs.

What's the difference between solid and engineered hardwood?

Solid hardwood is made from a single piece of wood and can be refinished multiple times. Engineered hardwood has a plywood core with a hardwood veneer, making it more stable for areas with moisture or temperature fluctuations.

Do you provide warranties on your work?

Yes, we provide a 5-year craftsmanship warranty on all installations and refinishing work. This covers installation quality, workmanship, and any issues related to our installation process.

Can you refinish existing hardwood floors?

Absolutely! We can refinish most hardwood floors to restore their original beauty. We'll assess the condition during our consultation and let you know if refinishing is the best option or if replacement is recommended.

What areas do you serve for flooring services?

We serve Rochester Hills, Troy, Auburn Hills, Birmingham, Bloomfield Hills, and surrounding areas within a 30-mile radius of 48307. Contact us to confirm service availability in your area.
